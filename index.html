About Me

Dr. Raether is a graduate of the Chicago School of Professional Psychology, recently earning her Psy.D in School Psychology. Her dissertation evaluated what virtual techniques schools were using to help mitigate anxiety in students during the COVID-19 pandemic. She has extensive experience in the field both in schools and a variety of other domains. Most recently, she has been interning as a school psychologist at the Tuscon Unified School District.
